ZHEN HUAN

Zhen Huan is the heroine in the novel and TV series in "The Legend of Zhen Huan", written by Liu Lianzi and directed by Zheng Xiaolong, which depicts a girl's legendary life and love story after married to an emperor.

Three reasons I admire the character:

  • She slays! Zhen Huan is intelligent and aspirant. She knows deeply what she want to achieve her life goal.
  • Zhen Huan fights for justice and punishes those villains.
  • She knows how to manipulate men's power and wealth.
